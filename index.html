Our Story

Where Heritage
Meets Innovation

Restaurant Mazen Chef is not simply a place to eat — it is a destination. Founded by the visionary Chef Mazen, the restaurant draws deeply from the rich culinary heritage of Syria, weaving it seamlessly with bold international influences.

Every evening, guests witness the magic unfold in real time through our signature live cooking show. The open kitchen becomes a stage, the sizzle of spices fills the air, and the boundary between diner and artist dissolves.

Whether you join us in Tangier overlooking the sea or in the heart of Rabat, expect nothing less than an extraordinary evening of flavors, aromas, and memories.
